Help and Support Resources for CircleCI

Contact our Support Team

Engineering support is available for all customers on paid plans. Submit a ticket from within the application by clicking the 'question mark' icon at the bottom right of the screen. If you're unable to access the application, email support@circleci.com.

Read more about our Support service

Community Forum - Discuss

All customers, whether on free or paid plans, have access to our Community Forum. Please search Discuss before contacting support as many common problems have solutions posted there.

The forum has an Announcements category that you can subscribe to for important news and updates.

Premium Support

For teams who need predictably rapid response times on their support tickets and preferential escalation of critical issues, we offer Premium Support packages with guaranteed response times and access to our Success Engineers.

Documentation

Documentation is arranged in sections for CircleCI 1.0, CircleCI 2.0, Enterprise 'Behind the Firewall', and API. There is a search bar at the top of each documantation page which searches the current section.

Use the dropdown selector in the left sidebar to choose a section.

Changelog

The changelog lists significant updates to our platform. It can be useful to check and see if a recent update could be the cause of a problem.

CircleCI Status

You can check the status of our service and our partners on the status page. We also report incidents using a banner in the application while an event is current.
